Travel Gallery - Peru

Sheila Géa

1. Lima - Peru

We started the trip through Lima, the capital of Peru. It was there where I ate the best ceviche of my life!

2. Huacachina - Peru

The second place was in Huacachina. The village is an oasis in the middle of the desert!

3. Cusco - Peru

The third place was Cusco, the city is beautiful. There the high altitude brought a bit of a headache, and that was when I chewed coca leaves for the first time. It is very bitter!

4. Machu Picchu - Peru

Then we went to the magical Machu Picchu! What a beautiful place, good energy, and amazing view of the mountains! I LOVED! In addition to visiting the ruins, we ventured up the Montaña Machu Picchu.

5. Ilhas Flutantes de Uros - Puno - Peru

Last but not least we went to Puno, there we visit the Ilhas Flutantes de Uros, these islands are on Lake Titicaca. Its artificial islands are made with a species of native vegetation, each island belongs to a family. We were able to enter in the islands, visit the residents' house, which is also made with this same vegetation, and we learned how everything works.
